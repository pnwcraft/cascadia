Deck repair, resurfacing, railing, and stair upgrades in Seattle.

Extend the life of an existing deck with targeted repairs, safer rails, stronger stairs, improved drainage, and new walking surfaces.

Repair scope

Fix the problems that matter first.

Seattle decks often show wear at rail posts, stair stringers, ledger connections, shady boards, fasteners, and areas where leaves hold moisture. Cascadia Deck & Fence helps homeowners decide whether a repair, resurfacing package, or full rebuild is the best use of budget.

Safety repairs

Loose rails, soft boards, stair movement, failing fasteners, and rot-prone details should be reviewed before cosmetic upgrades.

Resurfacing

Replace worn deck boards with cedar or composite when the framing is suitable and the layout still works for the home.

Upgrade paths

Add privacy screens, gates, lighting blocking, fascia, and modern railing systems while repair work is already underway.

When to call for deck repair

  • Boards feel soft, springy, or slippery.
  • Railings move when pushed.
  • Stairs pull away, sag, or show rot.
  • Water collects against the house or posts.
  • You want a maintenance plan before listing or buying a home.
