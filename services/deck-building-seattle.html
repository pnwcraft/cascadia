What we build

Cascadia Deck & Fence designs deck projects around how Seattle homeowners actually use outdoor space: morning coffee, grilling, dogs, kids, view corridors, hot tubs, and covered seating. Every estimate starts with access, slope, drainage, ledger attachment, flashing, railing height, stair layout, and material options.

  • Ground-level and raised backyard decks
  • Composite decking and hidden fastener systems
  • Western red cedar decks and picture-frame borders
  • Deck stairs, landings, guardrails, handrails, and gates
  • Waterproofing details, flashing, ventilation, and drainage planning
  • Permit-ready project scopes for structural deck work
