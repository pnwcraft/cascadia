What this form does

It is a client intake form, not a password account.

A static website cannot safely create private customer accounts without a backend. This registration form is ready for production lead intake and can later connect to a CRM, Airtable, HubSpot, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or a custom customer portal.

Best next production upgrade
